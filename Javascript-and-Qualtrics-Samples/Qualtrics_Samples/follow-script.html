Imagine that you are on a stage as a speaker. You are giving a presentation in front of a group of people. The group of people is attentively watching you talk, now. From behind the audience, a big television camera is videotaping you. Your image is being broadcast live online for viewers at another location. Gaze into the audience in front of you as well as the camera lens focusing on you, carefully. How are you presenting yourself to the audience? How does the audience see you? Pay close attention to the audience viewing you, and consider how you look in their eyes.
